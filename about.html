About Me

I am currently on a learning journey to become a professional developer.

I first had a glimpse of web development in 2011. Equipped with nothing but determination & Google, I was customising my Tumblr themes to fit the aesthetics & purpose of my blogs. These blogs are long gone, but my interest in coding was forever piqued, although I decided to pursue other endeavours.

Fast forward (almost) a decade later to 2020, COVID-19 rocked the globe, some of us had to re-evaluate our lives, and I was left with no clear direction with what I wanted pursue in mine. I took this time for personal development; I tried reverse-engineering my life vision and combining it with skills and interests that I already have. I have a knack for problem-solving, I like building things, I like renovations; before & after's, I like design. At the epicentre of all these things was coding, web development to be precise.

Two years later, I discovered and immersed myself in the SheCodes workshops, and since October 2022 I have been learning HTML, CSS & Javascript while building real-life applications/projects.
