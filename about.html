About Me

I first had a glimpse of web development in 2011. The typical story of a teenager equipped with nothing but sheer determination & Google, customising Tumblr themes for aesthetic purposes. These themes are now long gone, but my interest in coding was forever piqued.

Fast forward to more than a decade later, I decided to revisit and combine the skills and interests that I already have. I have a knack for problem-solving, I like building things, I like design, I like technology. At the epicentre of all these things was coding.

In October 2022, I immersed myself in the SheCodes workshops and have been learning and building applications/projects with the fundamentals and React ever since.

I am currently seeking new opportunities in front-end development. I am open to working remotely and have flexible hours.
